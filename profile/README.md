# Welcome to Skinnertopia

Skinnertopia is a company built around advancing artificial intelligence for all. Instead of gatekeeping models, we release them.

Our model series, GRaPE, is our primary work here at Skinnertopia. We work to improve these models the best we can, adding unique ways to make them better than other open models, and hoping to top benchmarks.

We work on products like Scribe and GRaPE Chat for you to use our models for chatting, and for work.

We open-source research and cookbooks for our products, so you can truly make it yours.
